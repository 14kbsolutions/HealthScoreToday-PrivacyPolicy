## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

When you create an account or use the App, we may collect certain personal information including:

* Name
* Email address
* Age
* Gender
* Height
* Weight
* Lifestyle habits (activity level, sleep habits, diet, smoking, alcohol consumption)

This information is used to calculate your **health score** and generate **personalized health insights and suggestions**.

---

### 2.2 Health and Lifestyle Data

The App may collect additional lifestyle and wellness information such as:

* Eating habits (fruits, vegetables, processed food, seafood, etc.)
* Physical activity levels
* Work habits
* Sleep quality
* Hobbies or recreational activities

This data is used solely to generate health recommendations and improve your experience in the App.

### 2.3 Images and Media

If you upload images (for example, food photos for calorie estimation), the App processes these images to analyze food content and nutritional estimates.

Images may be processed by third-party AI services for analysis. We do not use uploaded images for advertising purposes.

We may automatically collect limited technical data including:

* Device type
* Browser type
* IP address
* Application usage statistics
* Log data for security and debugging

This information helps us maintain performance, security, and reliability of the App.
